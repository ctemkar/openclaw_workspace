# trading_dashboard_monitor\n\n## Objective\nMonitor http://localhost:5001/ for trading logs, status updates, and risk parameters. Log all extracted data to `./trading_monitoring.log`.\n\n## Critical Alerts\nDetect and log any triggered stop-loss/take-profit orders or critical drawdown to `./critical_alerts.log`.\n\n## Summary\nProvide a plain text summary of the analysis and any critical alerts.\n\n## Schedule\nEvery 5 minutes, with a timezone of Asia/Bangkok.\n\n## Notes\nThis job is scheduled to run every 5 minutes. The agent will extract and log trading data, with a focus on critical events like stop-loss/take-profit orders or significant drawdowns. A summary will be generated, and critical alerts will be logged separately.
