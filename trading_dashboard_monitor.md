# trading_dashboard_monitor\nThis agent monitors the trading dashboard at http://localhost:5001/. \n\nIt extracts trading logs, status updates, and risk parameters, logging them to `trading_monitoring.log`.\n\nCritical events such as triggered stop-loss/take-profit orders or significant drawdowns are logged to `critical_alerts.log`.\n\nA plain text summary of the analysis and any critical alerts is provided.
